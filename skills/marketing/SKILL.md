## Product Positioning

### Value Proposition Formula

```
For [target customer]
Who [has this problem/need]
Our [product] is a [category]
That [key benefit]
Unlike [alternatives]
We [unique differentiator]
```

**Example - Theme:**
```
For small business owners
Who struggle to create professional websites without hiring developers
starter Theme is a conversion-focused WordPress theme
That helps you build a stunning website in under an hour
Unlike complex page builders
We provide pre-built industry templates with guided setup
```

**Example - Plugin:**
```
For WooCommerce store owners
Who lose sales due to complicated checkout processes
QuickCheckout is a checkout optimization plugin
That reduces cart abandonment by 40%
Unlike other checkout plugins
We use AI to auto-fill fields and predict user intent
```

## Copywriting Formulas

### AIDA
- **A**ttention: Hook with problem or curiosity
- **I**nterest: Explain the solution
- **D**esire: Show benefits and social proof
- **A**ction: Clear CTA

### PAS
- **P**roblem: Identify pain point
- **A**gitation: Make it worse
- **S**olution: Present your product

### BAB
- **B**efore: Current painful state
- **A**fter: Desired future state
- **B**ridge: Your product connects them

### 4Ps
- **P**roblem: What's wrong
- **P**romise: What you'll deliver
- **P**roof: Evidence it works
- **P**ush: CTA with urgency

### PASTOR
- **P**roblem: Identify the pain
- **A**mplify: Show consequences
- **S**tory: Share a relatable narrative
- **T**estimony: Social proof
- **O**ffer: Present your solution
- **R**esponse: Call to action

### FAB
- **F**eatures: What it has
- **A**dvantages: Why that matters
- **B**enefits: What they get
